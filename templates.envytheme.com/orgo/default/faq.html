  • Where is the location of the farmland?

    Iwo, Osun State.

  • What does the ₦1.5m entails?

    The ₦1.5m covers the cost of land, Agency and legal fee, professional survey, bulldozer clearing, farm layout, seedlings and other inputs, plating, irrigation, operations and management for the first year.

  • Any other hidden charges aside the ₦1.5m?

    No other charges.

  • Can I pay in installments?

    Yes, you can make an initial payment of ₦500,000 and the balance of ₦1m can either be paid once or installments of #500,000 each within four months.

  • Who owns the land?

    The land, the crops and whatever on it remains that of the buyer/ subscriber forever. The original documents of the land shall remain with him/ her, he/ she can process and have the C of O, do insurance of the farm or sell the farm whenever he/ she wishes.

  • What happens with the farm management after the first year?

    We will still provide management for the farm for subsequent years but a yearly management fee. The owner however has the liberty to hand over the management to another person or firm but be rest assured that we will offer efficient management at the most reasonable cost.

  • What about the recent Fulani Herdsmen and Farmers crisis all over, I hope it will not affect my farm?

    Farmers who plant crops which cows can feed on are majorly predispose to crisis with The Fulani Herdsmen. Cows cannot eat palm tree or plantain tree. This makes the two one of the least risky crop to plant for long term stable income.

  • Is the land genuine?

    Yes, the lands are genuine and were purchased from the direct source (family). Furthermore, we ensured that we obtain the signature of the king of each of the communities on the agreement for the lands bought so far.

  • Is the return on investment flat rate or subjected to variation?

    The return on investment is subject to variation according to the current market prices at the time of harvesting. However, we shall also be off taking the products for processing so you are sure of sales of your farm produce at the market price.
